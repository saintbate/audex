# Audex Demo Video — Shot List & Script

**Target length:** 60-90 seconds
**Tool:** QuickTime screen recording (Cmd+Shift+5 on Mac) or Loom
**Style:** No face cam needed. Screen recording with voiceover. You can record the voiceover separately and overlay it.

---

## Shot 1: The Hook (0:00 - 0:10)

**Show:** Terminal with the engine running — specifically the output of a company being analyzed, claims being extracted, checks being run.

**Voiceover:**
> "Every quarter, public companies file hundreds of pages with the SEC. Nobody reads them all. We built an AI that does — and checks every number."

**Tip:** Use the output from a real analysis run. Even a screenshot of the terminal scrolling with pass completions and check counts looks impressive.

---

## Shot 2: The Engine Output (0:10 - 0:20)

**Show:** Terminal output showing a company completing analysis — the moment the signal appears:

```
[SRE] Pass 4 complete — anomaly=57 quality=9 momentum=21 signal=strong_sell
```

**Voiceover:**
> "Our engine runs four passes on each filing — extracting claims, cross-referencing numbers, checking trends across years, and scoring anomalies. Sempra Energy just scored a 57 out of 100 on our anomaly scale."

---

## Shot 3: The Website — Leaderboard (0:20 - 0:30)

**Show:** Open audex.tech/leaderboard. Slowly scroll down the ranked table. Pause on the top entries (SRE, D, VLO).

**Voiceover:**
> "Results go live on our leaderboard. 40 companies ranked by anomaly score. Nine came back Strong Sell."

---

## Shot 4: The Company Page (0:30 - 0:45)

**Show:** Click on $SRE from the leaderboard. Show the company report page — the anomaly gauge, quality gauge, key findings.

**Voiceover:**
> "Each company gets a full report. Sempra's net income differs from their own XBRL data by $122 million. Revenue discrepancy of $440 million. Quality score: 9 out of 100 — the worst in our universe."

---

## Shot 5: The Track Record (0:45 - 0:55)

**Show:** Navigate to audex.tech/track-record. Show the backtest table with the signal performance data.

**Voiceover:**
> "This isn't theoretical. Backtested across 97 companies and four market regimes, our sell signals average negative 9.5% alpha versus the S&P. Buy-sell spread of 23 percentage points."

---

## Shot 6: The CTA (0:55 - 1:05)

**Show:** Scroll to the newsletter signup on the landing page. Type in an email and hit subscribe (use a test email).

**Voiceover:**
> "Every Monday, subscribers get the five riskiest filings from the past week — free. The numbers should add up. We check if they do. Audex dot tech."

---

## Production Tips

1. **Record at 1440p or higher** — grant reviewers watch on large screens
2. **Use the dark site as-is** — it looks great on screen recordings, no extra styling needed
3. **Slow mouse movements** — don't rush. Let each page breathe for 2-3 seconds
4. **Record audio separately** if your mic picks up typing/clicking sounds. Use Voice Memos on Mac, then combine in iMovie
5. **No music needed** — clean voiceover over screen recording is more professional for grant applications
6. **Export as MP4, 1080p** — works everywhere (YC application, SBIR upload, X post)

## Quick Record Checklist

- [ ] Close all other browser tabs (clean URL bar)
- [ ] Use Chrome/Arc in full screen mode
- [ ] Have the terminal ready with a recent analysis output (or scroll through the saved terminal log)
- [ ] Navigate: Terminal → Leaderboard → Company Page → Track Record → Landing Page signup
- [ ] Total screen time: aim for 60 seconds of footage, you can always trim
- [ ] Record voiceover after if you want it cleaner
